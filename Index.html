About Me

Hello! I'm Alex Morgan, a passionate web designer and developer with over 5 years of experience creating digital solutions that help businesses thrive online.

My journey began with a degree in Graphic Design, but I quickly discovered my love for the intersection of design and technology. Since then, I've worked with startups, small businesses, and large corporations to create websites that are not only visually appealing but also highly functional and user-friendly.

I believe that great design should solve problems, tell stories, and create meaningful connections between brands and their audiences. My approach combines strategic thinking with creative execution to deliver results that exceed expectations.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical website project take?

The timeline for a website project varies depending on the scope and complexity. A basic website typically takes 2-4 weeks, while more complex projects with custom functionality can take 6-8 weeks or longer. I provide a detailed project timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you provide website maintenance after launch?

Yes, I offer ongoing maintenance packages to ensure your website remains secure, up-to-date, and performing optimally. These packages include regular updates, security monitoring, backups, and technical support.

What platforms do you work with?

I have experience with various platforms including WordPress, Shopify, Webflow, and custom solutions. I'll recommend the platform that best suits your specific needs, budget, and technical requirements.

Can you help with existing website redesigns?

Absolutely! I frequently work with clients to redesign and improve existing websites. This process typically involves evaluating the current site, identifying areas for improvement, and implementing a new design that better aligns with your goals.

What is your payment structure?

I typically require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the remaining 50% due upon project completion. For larger projects, I can arrange a payment plan with milestones. All payment details are clearly outlined in our project agreement.
